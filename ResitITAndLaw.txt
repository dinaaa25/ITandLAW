"
Does sui generis protect the contents of the database?	Investment content protection: Yes, it does if you have made substantial investment in creation of DB via Obtaining, Verification or Presentation of the database contents to prevent extraction and or reutilization of the whole or substantial parts of the database contents quantitatively/qualitatively. 
What does sui generis not protect?	Sui generis does not protect the creation of the materials which make up the contents. Sui generis protects the investment.
What is substantial investment?	Obtaining, Verification or Presentation of Data. For example: You had to obtain data by paying someone, so this way you can say you did substantial investment ( no creativity but substantial investment means DB protected under Sui generis)
